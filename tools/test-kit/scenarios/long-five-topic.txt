ALEX: It's not. For someone in your income bracket I usually recommend supplementing with an individual policy that brings total coverage closer to seventy percent and has a thirty day elimination. The cost would be around two hundred dollars a month.

DANIEL: She mentioned something about wanting to give some of it away to charity.

ALEX: Then a qualified charitable distribution is exactly what we want. We can have up to one hundred thousand a year sent directly from her IRA to qualified charities and it counts toward the RMD without showing up as income.
