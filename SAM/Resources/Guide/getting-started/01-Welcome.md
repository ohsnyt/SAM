# Welcome to SAM

SAM is your cognitive business and relationship coaching assistant. It helps you build your financial practice by coaching you on two levels simultaneously.

## Relationship Coaching

SAM observes your interactions — meetings, calls, emails, notes — and recommends specific actions for each person in your network. It tells you who to follow up with, what to say, and why it matters. Every suggestion includes copy-paste-ready text, not vague advice.

## Business Strategy

SAM reasons across all your relationships, pipeline stages, production metrics, and time allocation to guide the growth of your practice. It connects individual actions to business-level goals, so a follow-up call isn't just relationship maintenance — it's pipeline velocity.

## Everything On-Device

All AI processing happens on your Mac using Apple's on-device models. No data leaves your computer. No cloud AI, no telemetry, no third-party services.

## Getting Started

1. **Grant permissions** — SAM needs access to Contacts, Calendar, and optionally Mail and Messages. You'll be prompted during onboarding, or configure them in Settings > Permissions.
2. **Import your contacts** — SAM integrates with Apple Contacts. Choose a contact group in Settings > Data Sources > Contacts, and SAM imports the people you work with.
3. **Check Today** — Your daily briefing shows priorities, upcoming meetings, and coaching suggestions. This is your starting point each day.
4. **Take notes** — After meetings or calls, capture notes on a person's profile. SAM analyzes them for action items, relationships, and life events.
5. **Set goals** — Define targets in the Business dashboard so SAM can track your pace and connect daily actions to quarterly objectives.

## Finding Help

- **Tips** appear contextually near features during your first days — they include "Learn more" links to this guide
- **"?" buttons** in toolbars and headers open the relevant guide article for that view
- **Help > SAM Guide** (or **⌘?**) opens this guide anytime
- **Settings > Guidance & Tips** lets you toggle tips, help buttons, and reset all tips to see them again
