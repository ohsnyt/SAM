# SAM Event Management — Overview

## What This Feature Does

SAM's Event Management system helps you plan, promote, and run recurring workshops, seminars, and client events — all from within SAM. Instead of juggling spreadsheets, email threads, and sticky notes to track who's been invited, who RSVP'd, and who showed up, SAM handles it in one place.

## The Big Picture

There are two main areas inside Events:

1. **Events** — where you create and manage individual workshops, track invitations and RSVPs, and communicate with participants
2. **Presentations** — a library of your reusable presentation decks (PDFs) that SAM can read, summarize, and link to events

## What SAM Does Automatically

- **Drafts personalized invitations** for each participant based on their relationship with you
- **Monitors your messages and emails** for RSVP responses and updates participant status automatically
- **Detects when someone new says they're coming** and adds them to the participant list (flagged for your review)
- **Notices when someone says they're bringing guests** and tries to match named guests to your contacts
- **Reads your presentation PDFs** and extracts key talking points and summaries you can use for marketing
- **Signs messages with your name** and learns your preferred closing style over time

## Where to Find It

In SAM's sidebar, click **Events** to open the Event Manager. You'll see a segmented control at the top to switch between **Events** and **Presentations**.
