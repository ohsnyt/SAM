# Undoing Mistakes

## Deleting an Event

If you accidentally delete an event, don't panic. SAM keeps a 30-day undo history.

When you delete an event:
1. The event disappears from the list
2. A toast notification appears at the bottom of the screen: **"Deleted: Personal Finance Workshop — Undo"**
3. Click **Undo** to restore the event immediately

## What Gets Restored

When you undo a deletion, SAM restores:
- All event details (title, description, date, venue, etc.)
- All participants (re-linked to the event)
- Auto-acknowledgment settings

**What's not restored:** Individual message history and RSVP detection metadata are not preserved through an undo. Participants come back with their default status, so you may need to re-confirm RSVP statuses.

## Undo History

Even if you miss the toast notification, you can find your deleted event in the undo history for up to 30 days. Check SAM's undo history to restore it later.
