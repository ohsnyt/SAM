# Promoting Your Event on Social Media

## Using the Promote Button

SAM can help you draft social media posts to promote your event across different platforms.

1. Click the **Promote** button near the top of the event detail view
2. A promotion sheet opens with platform options:
   - **LinkedIn** — Professional tone, networking-focused
   - **Facebook** — Casual, community-oriented
   - **Instagram** — Short, visual-friendly caption
   - **Substack** — Newsletter-style announcement
3. SAM drafts a post tailored to each platform's style and audience
4. Edit the draft to add your personal touch
5. Copy and paste into the platform to post
6. Mark as posted — SAM tracks which platforms you've promoted on

## Why Platform-Specific Drafts Matter

Each platform has a different audience and communication style. SAM adapts:

- **LinkedIn:** Emphasizes professional development, career relevance, and networking opportunities
- **Facebook:** More conversational, community-focused, includes logistics clearly
- **Instagram:** Punchy, short, designed to work as a caption with a visual
- **Substack:** Newsletter format, more detailed, educational framing

## Tips

- **Post 2-3 weeks before the event** for best reach
- **Post again 3-5 days before** as a reminder
- **After the event**, consider a recap post to build momentum for the next one
- SAM tracks your posting activity so it can remind you when it's time to promote
