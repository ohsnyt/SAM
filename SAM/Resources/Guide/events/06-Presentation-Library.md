# The Presentation Library

## What It Is

The Presentation Library is where you store your reusable presentation decks — the workshops and seminars you deliver repeatedly. Instead of hunting through folders for your slides, you keep them in SAM where they're organized, searchable, and connected to your events.

## How to Access It

1. Open the **Event Manager** (sidebar > Grow)
2. Click the **Presentations** tab at the top (next to Events)
3. You'll see your presentation library with a list on the left and details on the right

## Creating a Presentation Entry

### Method 1: Drag and Drop (Recommended)

The fastest way to add a presentation:

1. Open the **Presentations** tab
2. Find your PDF file in Finder
3. **Drag the PDF** onto the presentations list area
4. You'll see a blue dashed border appear — drop the file
5. SAM automatically:
   - Creates a new presentation entry
   - Names it based on the file name
   - Starts reading and analyzing the PDF content
   - Shows a "digesting" indicator in the bottom-left corner

**Look for the minion!** While SAM is reading your PDF, you'll see a small animated row in the sidebar footer that says "Extracting" and then "Analyzing" with the presentation title. This is SAM's way of telling you it's working on it in the background. You can keep using SAM normally — it won't slow anything down.

### Method 2: New Presentation Button

For more control over the details:

1. Click the **+ New** button
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Title** — Name of the presentation
   - **Description** — What it covers
   - **Duration** — How long it typically takes (in minutes)
   - **Target Audience** — Who it's designed for
   - **Topic Tags** — Keywords for categorization
3. In the **Files** section, click **Add PDF** to attach your slide deck
4. Click **Create**
5. SAM automatically starts analyzing the attached PDF

## What SAM Extracts

When SAM digests your presentation PDF, it creates:

| Output | What It Is | How You Can Use It |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Content Summary** | A 2-4 sentence overview of the presentation | Marketing copy, event descriptions |
| **Key Talking Points** | 5-10 specific points from the content | Social media posts, invitation text, pre-event prep |
| **Topic Tags** | Keywords for categorization | Searching, matching to events, content planning |

## Viewing the Analysis

After SAM finishes digesting (usually 30-60 seconds):

1. Click on the presentation in the list
2. The detail view shows:
   - Title and description
   - Attached files
   - **Content Summary** — SAM's overview of what the presentation covers
   - **Key Talking Points** — The main points SAM identified
   - **Topic Tags** — Categories SAM assigned
   - **Delivery History** — How many times you've delivered it, when it was last given, and when it's next scheduled

## Re-Analyzing

If you update your slides and want SAM to re-read them:
1. Open the presentation detail
2. Click the **Re-analyze** button
3. SAM reads the file again and updates the summary, talking points, and tags
