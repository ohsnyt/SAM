# Adding Participants to Your Event

## How to Add People

1. Open your event from the event list
2. Click the **Add People** button near the top of the detail view (above the participant filter)
3. A search sheet appears — type a name to search your SAM contacts
4. Click on a person to add them as a participant
5. Close the sheet when you're done

Each person you add starts with:
- **RSVP Status:** Pending (they haven't been invited yet)
- **Invite Status:** Not Invited
- **Priority:** Standard

## Participant Priority Levels

You can set priority levels that affect how SAM handles each participant:

| Priority | Who It's For | Auto-Acknowledgment |
|----------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Standard** | General attendees | Yes (if enabled) |
| **Key** | Referral partners, high-value prospects | No — personal touch preferred |
| **VIP** | Speakers, co-hosts, special guests | No — always personal |

**Why it matters:** If you turn on auto-acknowledgment for an event, SAM will only auto-send brief "Thanks, see you there!" replies to Standard participants. Key and VIP participants always get personal responses from you.

## SAM Can Suggest Participants

Click the **SAM Suggest** option and SAM will analyze your contacts to recommend people who might be a good fit for this event — based on their role, interests, and relationship history.

## What You'll See

Once participants are added, the detail view shows each person with:
- Their name and role badge (Client, Lead, Agent, etc.)
- Current RSVP status (color-coded)
- Invite status
- Quick action buttons for each participant

You can filter the participant list by RSVP status using the filter bar: **All**, **Accepted**, **Tentative**, **Declined**, **Pending**.
