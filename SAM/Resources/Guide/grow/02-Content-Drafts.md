# Content Drafts

SAM helps you create social media content based on your recent client interactions and business themes. Every draft is platform-aware, compliance-scanned, and ready to post.

## Creating a Draft

1. Click a **content idea** in the Grow > Content tab or in Strategic Insights
2. The Content Draft Sheet opens with the topic and key points pre-filled
3. Select your target platform:

| Platform | Draft Style |
|----------|------------|
| **LinkedIn** | 150–250 words, professional tone, hashtags, hook question, call-to-action |
| **Facebook** | 100–150 words, conversational, personal, engagement question |
| **Instagram** | 50–100 words, hook-first, 5–10 hashtags, line breaks for readability |
| **Substack** | 600–1,000 words, long-form newsletter with sections, personal anecdotes, examples |

4. Click **Generate Draft** — SAM creates a platform-specific draft using on-device AI
5. Review and edit the draft in the text area
6. Click **Regenerate** if you want a different version

## Topic Suggestions

SAM suggests content topics based on:

- Recent client questions and concerns from your meetings
- Industry trends relevant to your practice
- Themes from your notes and interactions
- Seasonal financial planning topics

Topics come from the Strategic Coordinator's content analysis and appear both in the Grow section and in Strategic Insights.

## Compliance Scanning

SAM automatically scans every draft for compliance-sensitive language:

- **Claims about returns** — "guaranteed 8% return"
- **Guarantees** — "you will never lose money"
- **Comparative statements** — "better than any bank product"

Flagged phrases appear with orange warning icons and suggestions for safer alternatives. Review these before posting.

## Posting and Logging

When your draft is ready:

- **Copy to clipboard** — Paste into the social platform of your choice
- **Log as Posted** (⌘Return) — Records the post in SAM for posting streak tracking and content cadence analysis
- **Cancel** (Esc) — Discard the draft

## Writing Voice

SAM learns your writing voice from your imported social posts (LinkedIn articles, Substack newsletters, Facebook posts). Over time, generated drafts match your natural tone and style.

## Tips

- Start with the AI draft, then personalize with a specific client story or recent example
- The Substack format generates full newsletter-length articles — great for repurposing across shorter platforms
- Review compliance flags seriously — they exist to protect your practice
- Log posts even if you edit them heavily, so SAM can track your content cadence accurately
