# Lead Acquisition

The Grow section helps you find new leads and grow your practice through social platform analysis, content creation, and audience insights.

## Two Tabs

The Grow dashboard has two tabs:

### Profile Tab — Social Platform Analysis

For each connected platform (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, Substack, X), you see an expandable card with:

- **Overall score** (0–100) with a color-coded ring:

| Score Range | Label | Color |
|------------|-------|-------|
| 76–100 | Strong Profile | Green |
| 61–75 | Good Progress | Yellow |
| 41–60 | Room to Grow | Orange |
| 0–40 | Needs Attention | Red |

- **What's Working Well** — Specific strengths SAM identified in your profile
- **Suggested Improvements** — Priority-ranked recommendations with actionable examples
- **Content Strategy** — Posting frequency, content mix, engagement assessment, topic suggestions
- **Network Health** — Growth trends, endorsements, audience reach
- **Changes Since Last Analysis** — Green checkmarks for improvements, orange marks for declines

### Content Tab — Strategy and Ideas

Shows your posting activity across platforms:

- **Days since last post** per platform (green if <7 days, orange if <14, red if >14)
- **Post count** this month
- **Weekly posting streak** with a flame icon
- **Quick log** — Record a post you made (select platform, enter topic, click "Log Post")
- **Content ideas** from the Strategic Coordinator — click any topic to open the Content Draft Sheet

## Analyzing Your Profiles

Click **Re-Analyze** in the toolbar to trigger a fresh analysis of your social profiles. SAM analyzes your LinkedIn and Substack profiles in parallel using on-device AI.

Analysis results include a **"Get Deeper Insights"** section with an external AI prompt you can copy to your clipboard for more detailed analysis in other tools.

## How Lead Acquisition Works

1. **Import social data** — CSV exports from LinkedIn, Facebook, or Substack (see **Social Imports** guide)
2. **SAM matches contacts** — Imported connections are matched against your Apple Contacts
3. **Matched contacts flagged** — People already in your network are highlighted as potential leads
4. **New contacts available** — Unmatched people can be added to SAM for tracking
5. **Quality analysis** — Over time, SAM identifies which sources produce the highest-quality leads based on conversion history

## Tips

- Keep your posting streaks going — consistent content builds audience trust
- Use the profile score to track improvement over time
- Content ideas from the Strategic tab are based on your actual client interactions, making them authentic and relevant
- Re-analyze after making profile updates to see your score change
