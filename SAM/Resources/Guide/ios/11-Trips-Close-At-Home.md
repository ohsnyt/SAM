# Closing a Trip at Home

If you've set a home address in Trip Settings, SAM offers a single-tap **Close at Home** action that ends the trip with home as the final stop. It's the fastest way to wrap up a day of field work.

## How to Use

1. Set your home address once in **Settings → Trip Settings**.
2. Start a trip when you leave; track stops as you go.
3. When you pull into your driveway, open the Trips tab.
4. Tap **Close at Home** (the prominent button below the standard Stop control).
5. SAM ends the trip, marks the final leg as a return-home segment, and opens the trip summary.

## What Close at Home Does

- Ends location tracking immediately.
- Logs your home location as the trip's last stop.
- Does **not** treat that final leg as a business stop — home is excluded from business miles.
- Opens the trip summary so you can confirm vehicle, purpose, and tap **Confirm Trip**.

## Tips

- The button only appears when a home address is set. Without one, use the regular **Stop** control and confirm in the summary.
- Close at Home is great for end-of-day wrap-up; for one-off errands mid-trip use **Add Stop** instead.
- The home address never leaves your iPhone — it's stored in Trip Settings on-device.
- Commuting trips (home → regular office → home) should still be marked **Commuting Trip** in the summary so they're excluded from your deduction.
