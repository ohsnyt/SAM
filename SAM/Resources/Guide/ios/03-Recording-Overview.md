# Recording Meetings on iPhone

The Record tab captures meeting audio in the field. When your Mac is reachable on the same Wi-Fi, audio streams live so the summary lands in seconds. When it isn't, the recording is saved locally and synced when you're home.

## How to Use

1. Tap the **Record** tab.
2. If a calendar event is starting soon, you'll see its title and attendees pre-loaded. Otherwise, pick a **Recording Type** (Client Meeting, Training/Lecture, or Board Meeting).
3. Set the **participant count and names** (see "Setting Participants Before Recording").
4. Tap the large **red record button** to start. The button works whether or not the Mac is connected.
5. While recording, you can **Pause**, **Resume**, or **Stop**. Audio level appears as a glow around the button.
6. After **Stop**, SAM finalizes and shows the summary (or a "Generating summary…" state while it travels back from the Mac).
7. Tap **Done** to sign off, or **Delete** to discard. Once you tap Done, raw audio and verbatim transcript are purged from the Mac.

## What You See

- **Connection pill** — "Searching for Mac…" while browsing for the desktop on Bonjour. Empty when connected.
- **Upcoming meeting banner** — shows the calendar event title when SAM auto-matched one.
- **Participant list** — tap to edit names, swipe-left to remove (minimum one).
- **Pending Recordings banner** — appears when local recordings are waiting to sync.

## Tips

- The record button never blocks waiting for the Mac. If the Mac isn't found, recording starts locally and uploads later.
- If the phone gets stuck on "Generating summary…" because the Mac dropped offline, **pull down to refresh** — that clears the stale waiting state.
- For Training/Lecture, no participant list is shown — SAM treats it as a solo recording and extracts key points and learning objectives instead.
