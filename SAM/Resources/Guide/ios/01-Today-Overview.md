# Today on iPhone

The Today tab on SAM Field is your morning snapshot in the field — the briefing pushed from your Mac, today's calendar, pending follow-ups, recent voice notes, and quick stats. It's designed to be glanceable on the way into a meeting, not a place to do deep work.

## How to Use

1. Open the **Today** tab — it's the leftmost icon in the tab bar.
2. Read the **Morning Briefing** at the top (synced from your Mac via iCloud — see "Briefing Sync to iPhone").
3. Scroll the **Schedule** section for the next events on your calendar.
4. Tap **Settings** in the top-right to manage Mac pairing or trip preferences.
5. **Pull down to refresh** to fetch the latest briefing, workspace settings, and calendar.

## What You See

- **Morning Briefing** — the narrative summary from your Mac, with a "minutes ago" timestamp so you know how fresh it is.
- **Unconfirmed Trips banner** — appears when you have driven trips that need IRS confirmation.
- **Active Trip banner** — shown while a trip is being tracked.
- **Schedule** — calendar events for today, with location and duration.
- **Follow-ups** — pending action items (overdue ones in red).
- **Recent Voice Notes** — captures from the last few days, with linked people.
- **Quick Stats** — meetings today, pending count, captures this week, and this-month business miles.

## Tips

- Priority actions and full follow-ups are intentionally suppressed on iPhone until they can be acted on directly (call, text, email). Use your Mac for triage.
- If "Calendar access needed" appears, tap **Enable Calendar Access** to grant permission via the system sheet.
- Trips counter only shows when business mileage exists for the current month — it stays out of the way otherwise.
