# Person Detail

Select a person to see their complete profile, coaching summary, interaction history, and everything SAM knows about your relationship.

## Header Section

The top of the detail view shows at a glance:

- **Photo and name** with organization and job title (from Apple Contacts)
  - **Drag & drop a photo** from Safari or any source onto the photo area to set it — the image is automatically cropped to square, resized, and saved to Apple Contacts
  - **Paste a photo** via right-click > Paste Photo when an image is on the clipboard
  - **One-click profile photo grab** — if no photo exists and the person has a LinkedIn or Facebook profile, click the photo placeholder to open their profile in a compact Safari window positioned next to SAM for easy dragging
    - **LinkedIn**: You can drag the profile photo directly from the page
    - **Facebook**: Click the friend's profile photo first to enlarge it, then drag the enlarged version
  - After a successful drop, the Safari window closes automatically while preserving your login session
- **Role badges** — click to edit, add custom roles, or remove roles
- **Health status** — "Healthy — last spoke 3 days ago" or "At risk — 28 days since last contact"
- **SAM Recommends** card — if there's an active coaching outcome for this person, it appears here with a direct link

## Quick Actions

A row of action buttons below the header gives you one-tap access to:

| Button | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **Text** | Send an SMS to their primary phone number |
| **Call** | Start a phone call |
| **Email** | Open a new email to their primary address |
| **LinkedIn** | Open their LinkedIn profile (if evidence-backed) |
| **Facebook** | Open their Facebook profile (if evidence-backed) |
| **Add Note** | Open the note capture area |

## Notes Section

An always-visible note capture area lets you record observations, meeting notes, and follow-ups. See the **Adding Notes** guide for full details on note capture, dictation, and AI analysis.

Below the capture area, your **note journal** shows all past notes in reverse chronological order with timestamps.

## Interaction History

A timeline of all linked evidence — emails, calendar events, messages, calls, and social interactions — sorted newest first. Each row shows:

- Source icon (color-coded by platform: email, calendar, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Event title and snippet
- Date

Click "Show more" to load additional history. The default view shows the 3 most recent interactions.

## Enrichment Banner

When SAM has found updated information from social imports (new email, phone, or job title), a blue banner appears: "X contact update(s) available." Click it to review and apply the updates to Apple Contacts.

## Recruiting Stage

For people with the Agent role, a 7-stage recruiting pipeline display shows their current position from Initial Conversation through Producing Agent, with mentoring cadence tracking.

## Production Records

For Clients and Applicants, a production section shows policies, products, carrier, premium, and status for each recorded transaction.

## More Details

Expand the "More Details" section to see:

- Additional phone numbers and email addresses
- Street addresses
- Websites and social profile links
- Who referred them
- Preferred communication cadence setting
- Lifecycle status controls

## Toolbar Actions

| Button | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **Open in Contacts** | Jump to this person in Apple Contacts |
| **Create in Contacts** | Create a new Apple Contact linked to this SAM person |
| **View in Graph** | Show this person in the Relationship Graph |
| **Actions menu** | Add to Context, Archive, Mark DNC, Mark Deceased, Refresh Contact |
