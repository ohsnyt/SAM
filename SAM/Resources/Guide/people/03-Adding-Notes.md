# Adding Notes

Capture meeting notes, observations, and follow-up items directly on a person's profile. Notes are SAM's richest source of relationship intelligence.

## Inline Note Capture

The note capture area is always visible on every person's detail view.

1. Click **"Add a note..."** to expand the editor
2. Type your notes — the editor supports **bold** (⌘B) and **italic** (⌘I) formatting
3. Optionally attach images by clicking the **paperclip** button or dragging files into the editor
4. Press **⌘S** or click **Save** when done

## Voice Dictation

For hands-free capture, click the **microphone** button:

1. Click the mic — it turns red and pulses while recording
2. Speak naturally, as long as you need
3. When you stop, SAM automatically **polishes** the transcript: cleaning up filler words, fixing grammar, and improving readability
4. Review the polished text — click **"Undo polish"** if you prefer the raw transcript
5. Save when ready

Dictation uses Apple's on-device speech recognition. No audio leaves your Mac.

## What SAM Does With Your Notes

After you save a note, SAM analyzes it on-device to extract:

| Extracted Data | Example |
|---------------|---------|
| **Action items** | "Follow up with proposal by Friday" |
| **Life events** | "Sarah mentioned her daughter's graduation" |
| **Topics** | Retirement planning, insurance review |
| **People mentioned** | "Her brother Tom might be interested" |
| **Relationships** | "Tom is Sarah's brother" — SAM maps this connection |

Extracted action items become coaching outcomes in your queue. Life events appear on the person's timeline and trigger outreach suggestions at the right time.

## Auto-Linking to Meetings

If you save a note within a short window after a calendar event ends, SAM automatically links the note to that meeting. You'll see a confirmation: "Linked to: Team Planning Meeting."

## Post-Meeting Capture Prompts

SAM watches your calendar and prompts you after meetings end. The prompt includes:

- Names of attendees from the meeting
- Open action items from pre-meeting preparation
- A ready-to-use note editor

This appears in the Today view's follow-up coach section. See the **Daily Briefing** guide for more on the Today view layout.

## Tips

- Capture notes right after meetings while details are fresh — SAM prompts you automatically
- Include who was there, what was discussed, and what you committed to
- Mention other people by name — SAM detects them and maps relationships
- Use dictation when you're between meetings and can't type
- Notes with specific commitments ("I'll send the proposal by Thursday") generate better coaching outcomes than vague summaries
