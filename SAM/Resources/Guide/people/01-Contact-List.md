# Contact List

The People section shows everyone SAM tracks, with health indicators, role badges, and quick access to coaching recommendations.

## Health Indicators

Each contact row shows a visual health status based on interaction velocity — not just how long since you last spoke, but whether the pace is accelerating or slowing:

| Indicator | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| **Green dot** | Healthy — recently engaged, on track |
| **Yellow dot** | Engagement slowing — velocity declining |
| **Orange strip** | Needs attention — approaching overdue |
| **Red strip** | At risk — significantly overdue for contact |

Contacts at risk show a coaching preview directly in the list: "Follow up — 12 days since last contact."

## Role Badges

Contacts are tagged with color-coded role badges that appear throughout SAM:

| Color | Role |
|-------|------|
| Green | Client |
| Yellow | Applicant |
| Orange | Lead |
| Purple | Vendor |
| Teal | Agent |
| Indigo | External Agent |
| Cyan | Referral Partner |

A person can have multiple roles — for example, a Client who is also a Referral Partner.

## Sorting

Use the sort menu in the toolbar to order the list by:

- **First Name** (default) — Alphabetical
- **Last Name** — Alphabetical by surname
- **Email** — Alphabetical by email address
- **Relationship Health** — Highest risk first, so the people who need your attention most are at the top

## Filtering

The filter menu offers two types of filters:

**Role filters** — Show only people with specific roles. You can select multiple roles (e.g., show all Clients and Leads together).

**Special filters** — Focus on specific situations:

| Filter | Shows |
|--------|-------|
| Pending Role Suggestions | People where SAM detected a likely role you haven't confirmed |
| Needs Contact Update | People with pending data from social imports |
| Not in Contacts | SAM people not yet linked to Apple Contacts |
| Archived | Contacts you've archived |
| Do Not Contact | Contacts marked DNC |
| Deceased | Deceased contacts |

Active filters appear as chips above the list. Click "Clear All Filters" to reset.

## Status Badges

Look for additional badges on contact rows:

- **Blue "Not in Contacts"** — This person exists in SAM but isn't linked to Apple Contacts
- **Blue "Updates available"** — Social import data is waiting for your review
- **Orange count** — Consent alerts requiring attention
- **Red count** — Review alerts requiring attention

## Importing Contacts

SAM syncs with a designated group in Apple Contacts. Use the **Import Now** button in the toolbar to trigger a manual sync. The toolbar also shows the last import status — how many contacts were imported and when.

Configure which contact group SAM uses in Settings > Data Sources > Contacts.

## Contextual Actions

Right-click any contact to access quick actions:

- **Archive** — Move to archived state (for contacts you no longer actively manage)
- **Reactivate** — Return an archived, DNC, or deceased contact to active status
