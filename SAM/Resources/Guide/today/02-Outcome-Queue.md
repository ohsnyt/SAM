# Outcome Queue

The outcome queue is SAM's prioritized list of coaching recommendations — specific actions to take with specific people, and why they matter.

## How Outcomes Are Generated

SAM continuously scans your data to generate outcomes. Sources include:

- **Upcoming meetings** — Preparation suggestions with talking points for meetings in the next 48 hours
- **Past meetings without notes** — Follow-up prompts reminding you to capture takeaways
- **Relationship health** — Outreach suggestions for people whose engagement is declining
- **Pending action items** — Follow-through on commitments from your notes
- **Pipeline stalls** — People stuck too long at a pipeline stage
- **Business goals** — Growth activities when your queue is thin and goals need attention
- **Content opportunities** — Social media post suggestions based on recent interactions
- **Life events** — Birthday, anniversary, or milestone outreach for specific people

## What Each Outcome Shows

| Element | What It Means |
|---------|--------------|
| **Kind badge** | Category: Preparation, Follow-Up, Proposal, Outreach, Growth, Content, etc. |
| **Priority dot** | Red = urgent, yellow = important, green = when you have time |
| **Title** | What to do and with whom |
| **Rationale** | Why this matters for the relationship or your business |
| **Suggested next step** | Copy-paste-ready text — a draft message, talking point, or script |
| **Deadline** | "Overdue," "3h left," or "2d left" when time-sensitive |
| **Sequence indicator** | "Step 2 of 5" for multi-step campaigns |

## Working the Queue

The Today view shows your **top 5 outcomes** to avoid overwhelm. Click "Show all" to see the full list.

For each outcome, you have three choices:

- **Done** — You acted on it. SAM may ask for a quick 1–5 star rating (this helps it learn).
- **Skip** — You're passing on this one. SAM records the signal for future calibration.
- **Mute kind** — Right-click the Skip button and choose "Stop suggesting [type]" to permanently suppress a category.

## Action Lanes

The primary action button on each outcome varies by type:

| Outcome Type | Button | What Happens |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| Preparation | Record | Opens quick note with meeting context prefilled |
| Follow-Up | Record | Opens note capture with attendee names |
| Outreach | Communicate | Opens compose window with draft message and channel recommendation |
| Content | Draft Post | Opens content draft sheet with topic and key points |
| Growth | Schedule | Navigates to the person's detail view |
| Setup | Open | Opens the relevant settings or guide page |

## Hero Card

The first outcome in the Today view is your **hero card** — SAM's single highest-priority recommendation, displayed larger with more detail. This is the one thing you should do first.

## Copy-Paste Ready

Every outcome includes a **suggested next step** with a copy button. Click it to copy the exact text to your clipboard — a complete draft message, not instructions on what to write.

## Compliance Scanning

For outreach and content outcomes, SAM scans AI-generated drafts for compliance-sensitive language (claims about returns, guarantees, comparative statements) and shows an orange warning flag if anything needs your review before sending.

## Adaptive Learning

SAM tracks which outcomes you act on and which you skip. Over time, it adjusts:

- **Outcome types** you frequently skip get lower priority
- **Times of day** when you're most productive influence scheduling
- **Encouragement style** adapts to what resonates with you

See Settings > AI & Coaching > "What SAM Has Learned" to review and adjust these preferences.
