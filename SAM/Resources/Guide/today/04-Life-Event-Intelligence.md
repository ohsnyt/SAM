# Life Event Intelligence

SAM detects life events from your notes and interaction history — birthdays, graduations, promotions, weddings, losses — and turns them into timely outreach opportunities with coaching support.

## How Life Events Are Detected

When you save a note or SAM analyzes interaction evidence, the AI identifies mentions of significant personal events:

| Event Type | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **Birthday** | "Sarah mentioned her birthday is next Friday" |
| **Graduation** | "Tom's daughter is graduating from college in June" |
| **Promotion** | "Just got promoted to VP of Sales" |
| **Wedding/Engagement** | "Getting married in September" |
| **New Baby** | "They're expecting their first child" |
| **Retirement** | "Planning to retire at the end of the year" |
| **Loss/Bereavement** | "His mother passed away last week" |
| **Health Issue** | "She mentioned her surgery is scheduled for next month" |
| **Move/Relocation** | "They're moving to Denver" |
| **Anniversary** | "20th wedding anniversary coming up" |

## Life Events in the Today View

Detected life events appear in the **Life Events** section of the Today view with a pink heart icon and a count badge. Each card shows:

- The person's name and role badge
- The event type and description
- An outreach suggestion

## Life Event Coaching Sessions

For events that benefit from thoughtful, personalized outreach, click **Get Coaching** to open a chat-style coaching session. SAM helps you:

- **Think through your response** — appropriate tone for the event type (empathy for losses, celebration for achievements)
- **Draft a message** — copy-paste-ready text for the right channel
- **Plan follow-up** — when to check in again after the initial outreach
- **Consider business implications** — some life events create planning opportunities (retirement, new baby, promotion)

The coaching adapts to the event type. A bereavement coaching session focuses on empathy and timing. A promotion coaching session might include congratulations and a natural segue to review their financial plans.

## Life Events Timeline

Each person's detail view includes a life events timeline showing all detected events in chronological order. This gives you a complete picture of what's happening in their life.

## Acting on Life Events

For each pending life event, you can:

- **Coach** — Open a coaching session for personalized guidance
- **Dismiss** — Mark as handled or not relevant

Acted-on events are recorded in the person's evidence history.

## Tips

- Mention life events in your notes by name — "Sarah's daughter Emily is graduating" gives SAM more to work with
- Life events detected from meetings and conversations are especially valuable because they show you're paying attention
- Some events (retirement, new baby) are natural opportunities for financial planning conversations — SAM's coaching helps you approach these sensitively
