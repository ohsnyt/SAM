# Daily Briefing

Your daily briefing is SAM's morning summary of what matters today. It appears automatically at the top of the Today view each morning and stays visible all day.

## What's Included

The briefing pulls together data from across SAM into a single, scannable summary:

| Section | What You See | Source |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| **Greeting** | "Good morning" with today's date | Always shown |
| **Narrative** | AI-written natural-language summary of your day | Generated from all sections below |
| **Calendar** | Meetings and events for the next 48 hours | Apple Calendar |
| **Follow-ups** | People who need attention based on interaction velocity | Relationship health engine |
| **Life events** | Birthdays, anniversaries, and milestones happening today | Detected from notes and evidence |
| **Strategic highlights** | Top 3 business recommendations from the Strategic Coordinator | Background analysis |
| **Goal pacing** | Alerts for goals that are at risk of falling behind | Business Goals |

## Interactive Checklist

Each briefing item has a **checkbox** so you can mark it handled as you work through your day. When you check off all items, SAM shows an "All done for today!" celebration banner.

Checked items persist for the day — you can leave SAM and come back without losing progress. The checklist resets each morning.

## Generating and Refreshing

SAM generates the briefing automatically on your first open each day. You'll see a progress bar as it gathers data and generates the narrative. The full process takes a few seconds.

To regenerate with the latest data, click **Refresh** in the toolbar. This is useful if your calendar changed or you completed actions since the morning.

## Monday Mornings

On Monday mornings, the briefing includes a **"This Week's Priorities"** section with goal pacing and strategic recommendations to start your week with clarity.

## Evening Recap

After about 5 PM, SAM offers an optional evening recap banner: "Ready for your end-of-day summary?"

The evening recap shows:

- **Meetings attended** and notes captured today
- **Outcomes completed** grouped by category
- **Streaks** you're maintaining (e.g., "3 days of morning prep")
- **Tomorrow preview** — what's on the calendar for tomorrow

You can **View** the full recap, postpone it with **Later**, or **Dismiss** it. On Fridays, the evening recap includes a "week so far" summary.

## Tips

- Check the briefing first thing each morning — it's designed to be your single starting point for the day
- Use the checkboxes to track what you've handled without leaving the Today view
- The narrative is generated by on-device AI and references your actual data — it's not generic advice
