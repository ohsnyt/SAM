# Business Dashboard Overview

The Business dashboard gives you a complete view of your practice. It's the command center for tracking goals, pipeline health, production, and strategic intelligence.

## Business Health Summary

At the top of the dashboard, four metric cards show your practice at a glance:

| Card | What It Shows |
|------|--------------|
| **Active Pipeline** | Total leads + applicants currently in your client funnel |
| **Clients** | Total active client count |
| **Recruiting** | Total people in your recruiting pipeline |
| **This Month** | Premium volume for the current month |

## Tabs

The dashboard is organized into tabs. Use the segmented picker to switch between them:

| Tab | Purpose | Guide Article |
|-----|---------|--------------|
| **Strategic** | AI-powered business intelligence, projections, and recommendations | Strategic Insights |
| **Pipeline** | Client funnel (Lead > Applicant > Client) and Recruiting funnel (7 stages) | Client Pipeline, Recruiting Pipeline |
| **Production** | Policies, products, premiums, and status tracking | Production |
| **Goals** | Business targets with pacing and progress tracking | Goals |
| **Mileage** | Business mileage rolled up from SAM Field trips, with IRS-ready totals and export | Mileage Tracking on iPhone |

For practices configured as non-financial, the Production tab is hidden (it's specific to insurance/financial product tracking).

## Refreshing Data

Click **Refresh** in the toolbar to update all pipeline metrics and regenerate strategic analysis. The Strategic tab shows "Last updated: X minutes ago" so you always know how fresh the data is.

Strategic analysis runs in the background using on-device AI. It may take a few seconds to regenerate after a refresh.

## Getting Started

If you're new to the Business dashboard:

1. **Set a goal** in the Goals tab — this gives SAM a target to pace you toward
2. **Check the Pipeline tab** to see your current funnel health
3. **Review Strategic Insights** for AI-generated recommendations based on your data
4. **Log production** in the Production tab to track policies and premiums
