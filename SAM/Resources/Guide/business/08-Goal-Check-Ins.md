# Goal Check-In Sessions

Goal check-ins are structured coaching conversations where you and SAM review progress on a specific goal, identify what's working, surface barriers, and commit to next steps. Each session produces a journal entry that builds a history of your goal journey.

## Starting a Check-In

1. Open the **Business** dashboard and go to the **Goals** tab
2. Find the goal you want to review
3. Click the **Check In** button (speech bubble icon) on the goal card

## The Check-In Conversation

The check-in opens a chat-style window with SAM. The conversation is goal-scoped — SAM has context about:

- Your goal's title, target, and deadline
- Current progress and pace status
- Your business profile and recent activity
- Previous check-in journal entries for this goal

SAM guides the conversation through key areas but follows your lead. A typical check-in covers:

| Area | What SAM Explores |
|------|------------------|
| **Progress review** | How things are going since the last check-in |
| **What's working** | Strategies and actions producing results |
| **Barriers** | What's slowing you down or blocking progress |
| **Strategy adjustment** | Whether the current approach needs changing |
| **Commitments** | Specific actions you'll take before the next check-in |

## Ending a Check-In

When the conversation reaches a natural conclusion, click **Done**. SAM summarizes the session into a structured **Goal Journal Entry** containing:

- **Headline** — A one-line summary of the check-in
- **What's working** — Strategies and actions that are producing results
- **What's not working** — Areas that need adjustment
- **Barriers** — Obstacles identified during the conversation
- **Adjusted strategy** — Any changes to approach (if discussed)
- **Key insight** — The most important takeaway from the session
- **Commitment actions** — Specific next steps you agreed to
- **Pace snapshot** — Your pace status at the time of check-in
- **Progress snapshot** — Your completion percentage at check-in time

## Goal Journal

Each goal maintains a journal of past check-in entries. Expand the **Journal** section on a goal card to see the history. Journal entries show:

- Date and headline
- Commitment actions (as a checklist reference)
- Pace status at the time

This creates an evolving record of your goal journey — what you tried, what worked, what didn't, and how your strategy evolved over time.

## Tips

- Check in on important goals weekly — regular reflection dramatically improves follow-through
- Be honest about barriers — SAM coaches better when it knows what's actually in the way
- Review past journal entries before starting a new check-in — they provide continuity
- Commitment actions from check-ins feed into SAM's coaching outcomes, so you'll see reminders to follow through
