# Recruiting Pipeline

Follow recruits through 7 stages from initial conversation to producing agent. This pipeline tracks the full WFG recruiting lifecycle.

## Stages

| # | Stage | What Happens |
|---|-------|-------------|
| 1 | **Initial Conversation** | First meeting or expression of interest |
| 2 | **Information Shared** | Opportunity materials and details provided |
| 3 | **Application Started** | Began the licensing application |
| 4 | **Licensed** | Obtained required licenses |
| 5 | **Contracted** | Signed with the organization |
| 6 | **In Training** | Completing initial training program |
| 7 | **Producing Agent** | Actively writing business |

## Funnel Visualization

A grid of bars shows the count at each stage with proportional heights. The funnel typically narrows from left to right — more people in early stages than later ones.

## Licensing Rate

A hero metric card shows your **licensing rate** — the percentage of recruits who reach the Licensed stage or beyond. This is one of the most important recruiting health indicators.

## Mentoring Alerts

SAM flags recruits who haven't had a mentoring check-in recently. The alert shows:

- Person's name and current stage
- How many days since last mentoring contact
- Color coding: **red** if more than 21 days overdue, **orange** if less

Click the **Log** button next to any alert to quickly record a mentoring touch. Click a person's name to navigate to their detail view.

## Why Mentoring Cadence Matters

Recruits who receive regular mentoring in their first months have significantly higher licensing and retention rates. SAM tracks this cadence automatically using evidence from your interactions (meetings, calls, messages, notes).

## Tips

- The licensing rate is your key metric — watch for trends over time
- Mentoring alerts in red need immediate attention: recruits who go too long without contact are at high risk of dropping out
- Stage transitions are logged immutably, so you can always see the historical path of each recruit
- Pair recruiting pipeline data with Strategic Insights to see patterns like "recruits who attend 3+ training sessions in month one have higher licensing rates"
