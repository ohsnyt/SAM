# Production Tracking

Log policies written, applications submitted, and products sold. SAM tracks status, product mix, and aging to give you a clear picture of your production output.

## Recording Production

Production records are entered by you — SAM never creates or modifies production data automatically. To add a record, enter it from a client's detail view. Each record captures:

| Field | What to Enter | Example |
|-------|--------------|---------|
| **Product type** | Life, health, annuity, etc. | Life Insurance |
| **Status** | Current state of the application | Pending |
| **Carrier** | Insurance company | Transamerica |
| **Premium** | Annual or monthly premium amount | $250/mo |
| **Submission date** | When the application was submitted | March 1, 2026 |
| **Issue date** | When the policy was issued (if applicable) | — |
| **Linked person** | The client this policy belongs to | Sarah Chen |

## Status Overview

At the top of the Production tab, status cards show your production by state:

- **Pending** — Submitted and awaiting processing
- **Applied** — Application received by carrier
- **Approved** — Policy approved and ready to issue
- **Issued** — Active policy
- **Lapsed** — Policy that has lapsed
- **Declined** — Application that was declined

Each card shows the count and total premium for that status.

## Product Mix

A breakdown by product type shows how your production is distributed — helping you see if you're diversified or concentrated in one area.

## Production Window

Use the segmented control to view production over **30, 60, 90, or 180 days**.

## Pending Submissions Alert

Applications that have been pending for an extended time appear in an orange alert section with:

- Person's name, carrier, and premium
- Days pending — **red** if over 30 days, **orange** otherwise

Click any pending item to navigate to that client's profile.

## All Production Records

Below the summary cards, a complete list shows every production record with person, product type, carrier, premium, status badge, and submission date. Click any record to navigate to the client.

## Data Integrity

Production data you enter is authoritative. SAM analyzes and reports on your records but never overrides, estimates, or fabricates production data. All calculations (totals, averages, trends) are computed in Swift from your actual entries.
