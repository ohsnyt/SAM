# Goals

Set business targets and track your progress with automated pacing, projections, and goal-connected coaching.

## Creating a Goal

1. Open the **Goals** tab in the Business dashboard
2. Click **+ Add Goal**
3. Fill in the goal details:

| Field | What to Enter | Example |
|-------|--------------|---------|
| **Title** | What you're aiming for | "Write 10 policies this quarter" |
| **Target value** | The number to hit | 10 |
| **Unit** | How to measure (count, currency, hours) | Count |
| **Category** | Production, recruiting, meetings, or custom | Production |
| **Start date** | When tracking begins | January 1, 2026 |
| **End date** | Deadline for the goal | March 31, 2026 |

4. Click **Create**

## Progress Tracking

Each goal card shows:

- **Progress bar** — Visual completion percentage
- **Current / Target** — Your actual numbers vs. the goal (formatted as currency, hours, or counts)
- **Days remaining** — Countdown to the deadline
- **Pace badge** — Color-coded status:

| Badge Color | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| **Green** | On pace or ahead |
| **Blue** | Slightly behind but recoverable |
| **Orange** | Falling behind — needs acceleration |
| **Red** | Significantly behind target |

## Pacing

SAM breaks your goal into weekly and daily targets based on your deadline. Below the progress bar, you'll see a pacing hint:

- "Need $2,500 per week to finish on time"
- "On pace for 12 of 10 target" (when you're ahead)

Projections use your historical pace to extrapolate — they're clearly labeled as estimates.

## Goal-Connected Coaching

When SAM generates coaching outcomes, it references your goals when relevant. A follow-up suggestion might note that completing it contributes to your Q2 policy target. This connects individual actions to your bigger-picture objectives.

Goal pacing alerts also appear in your morning briefing when a goal is at risk.

## Check-In Sessions

Each goal has a **Check In** button that opens a coaching conversation focused on that specific goal. Check-ins help you review progress, identify barriers, and commit to next steps. Each session produces a journal entry. See the **Goal Check-In Sessions** guide for details.

## Managing Goals

- **Edit** — Click the edit button on any goal card to change the target, dates, or category
- **Archive** — Archive completed or abandoned goals to keep your active list focused
