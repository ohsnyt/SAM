# Mac and iPhone Working Together

*Available on: Mac and iPhone*

A practice doesn't stop at your office door. You plan and review at your Mac, but you live the work in the field — meetings, drives, conversations between appointments. Most apps make you choose: full power on the desktop, or a stripped-down mobile companion. SAM treats your Mac and iPhone as one practice.

Setup is invisible. As long as both devices are signed into the same iCloud account, they recognize each other automatically. No pairing code, no QR scan, no fiddling with Bluetooth. Open SAM on your Mac and SAM Field on your phone, and they're already talking.

Each morning, your daily briefing — the rundown of what matters today, who you owe a follow-up, which life events are coming up — flows from your Mac to the Today tab on your phone. Pull to refresh for the latest. Step out for a meeting and everything you need is in your pocket.

When you record in the field, the audio streams to your Mac if you're on the same network — transcription, speaker separation, summary, and action item extraction run on your bigger machine. Out of range? Your phone records locally and syncs the moment you reconnect; your Mac processes it and pushes the summary back. Action items and life events SAM detects in the field show up on your Mac, ready to drive next week's coaching.

The benefit: zero friction between desk work and field work. The same practice, in your hand or on your screen.
