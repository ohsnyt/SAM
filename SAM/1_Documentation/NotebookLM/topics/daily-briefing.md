# Daily Briefing

Every morning, you face the same question: what should I focus on today? Scanning your calendar, checking old notes, and trying to remember who you owe a follow-up is a slow way to start.

SAM greets you each morning with a personalized briefing that pulls everything together in one view. You'll see a time-appropriate greeting, an AI-generated narrative summary of what matters today, and an interactive checklist of your calendar items. Below that, SAM surfaces follow-ups you need to make — each showing a badge with how many days it's been since your last contact, so you can feel the urgency at a glance.

If someone in your network has a life event coming up — a birthday, work anniversary, or recent milestone — SAM highlights it with a suggested outreach. Your active business goals appear with pacing indicators, and at-risk goals get called out so you can course-correct early in the week, not after it's too late.

As you work through items, check them off right in the briefing. Complete everything and SAM celebrates with a banner. There's also an optional evening recap for reviewing what you accomplished.

Below the briefing sits your outcome queue: a prioritized list of coaching actions SAM has generated for you. The highest-priority item appears as a hero card. Other important suggestions follow on.

With each suggestion, you can mark it Done, Skip it, or Mute that type entirely. A five-star rating option lets SAM learn what you value most — over time, it adapts to surface the coaching that actually moves your business forward.

SAM may suggest objectives that feel difficult. Try them. Ask for help from your colleagues. But if something isn't working for you, let SAM know.
