# Mileage Tracking

*Available on: iPhone (SAM Field)*

Independent strategists drive constantly — client meetings, prospect coffees, recruiting visits, networking events. The IRS wants a contemporaneous mileage log, but who has time to scribble odometer readings between appointments?

SAM Field handles it for you. Tap Start Trip when you head out and your phone tracks the route in the background. When you stop somewhere for more than a couple of minutes, SAM detects the dwell and logs it as a stop. Tag each stop with a purpose — client meeting, prospecting, recruiting, training, personal — and link it to the contact you visited. Need to add a stop on the fly? A map picker lets you drop a pin and resolve the address; a contact picker pulls from your SAM people, prioritizing today's calendar attendees and nearby contacts.

When you're done, tap Close at Home and SAM appends a final stop at your saved home address. Review the summary, pick the vehicle, mark commuting trips so they're flagged non-deductible, and confirm. That confirmation timestamp satisfies the IRS contemporaneous record requirement.

Filter your history by day, week, month, year, or all-time, with totals shown right in the footer. Swipe left to delete mistakes. Export the log as a CSV or PDF — the rate's applied at export time using the current IRS figure, since you plug it in at tax time anyway.

The outcome: a defensible mileage log that builds itself while you focus on the meeting, not the paperwork.
