# Event Management

Social media platforms are helpfull, but nothing beats meeting with people.

Hosting a client appreciation dinner or a recruiting seminar means juggling invitations, RSVPs, follow-ups, and presentations — usually across sticky notes and spreadsheets.

SAM manages the full event lifecycle in one place. Create an event with its format, dates, venue, and target attendance. Add participants and tag them by priority — Standard, Key, or VIP — so you know who gets the personal touch. SAM drafts personalized invitations for each attendee based on your relationship warmth: a long-time client gets a warmer, more personal invite than a recent lead.

As responses come in through email and messages, SAM automatically detects RSVPs with confidence scoring. You see at a glance who's confirmed, who's tentative, and who hasn't responded yet — without manually updating a list.

Need to promote the event on social media? SAM generates platform-specific posts for LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and Substack, each tailored to the right tone and length.

You will probably develop a handful of presentations that work really well. SAM maintains a library where you can put a PDF version of those presentations. SAM will analyze each one, generating summaries and key points to use in presentation-specific invitations and promotions. All invitations and follow-ups are personalize - no generic mail list blasts.

Since SAM knows your contacts, it weaves that knowlege into personalized invitations to events. If you mentioned that a client is aware of retirement issues, their invitation might just mention how that expertise will be relevant to this meeting!

Events flow through clear statuses — Draft, Inviting, Confirmed, In Progress, Completed — so you always know where things stand. After the event, participant data feeds back into your relationship records.

The result: you host polished, well-attended events without the administrative chaos, and every event strengthens your relationships and pipeline.
