# Time and Productivity

You feel busy all week, but when Friday hits, you wonder: did I actually spend my time on the things that grow my business?

SAM answers that question by categorizing every calendar event into one of ten categories: Client Meeting, Internal Meeting, Admin, Prospecting, Recruiting, Deep Work, Prep, Social, Content, and Other. It does this automatically using smart heuristics — a meeting with a client gets tagged as a Client Meeting, your weekly team call as Internal — and you can override any categorization with a click.

Your time allocation view shows the last seven days as a clear stacked bar chart, broken down by category. At a glance, you can see whether you spent enough hours in client-facing activities versus admin. SAM also tracks week-over-week trends for client meeting time, so you'll notice if the time you spend with clients is quietly shrinking.

Beyond the numbers, SAM spots calendar patterns you might miss: back-to-back meeting days that leave no prep time, your busiest day of the week, how many meeting-free deep work days you had, and whether your upcoming week looks heavier or lighter than usual.

SAM also tracks streaks — consecutive weeks of capturing meeting notes, contacting clients weekly, following up same-day, and posting content. These small habits compound into major results, and SAM makes sure you see your momentum.

The outcome: you stop guessing where your time goes and start deliberately investing it where it matters most for your practice.
