# Relationship Graph

You know your clients and recruits individually, but you've never been able to see how they all connect — who referred whom, which clusters of people know each other, and where the gaps in your network are.

SAM builds an interactive visual map of your entire professional network. Each person appears as a node, sized by their production value and colored by role: green for Clients, yellow for Applicants, orange for Leads, purple for Vendors, and teal for Agents. Lines between nodes show how people relate — solid lines for referrals, dashed for recruiting chains, and lighter connections for co-attendance at events or shared email threads.

SAM doesn't wait for you to draw these connections manually. It deduces relationships from evidence — shared meetings, email threads, family members mentioned in your notes — and prompts you to confirm or reject each one. Ghost nodes appear for people mentioned but not yet in your contacts, so you can spot opportunities you haven't pursued.

Toolbar controls let you filter by role, overlay health status or engagement velocity, and export the graph. You can multi-select nodes with shift-click or lasso, and double-click any person to jump straight to their detail view.

The real power of the Graph is pattern recognition. The graph reveals which referral chains are most productive, who bridges your separate network clusters, and where your coverage has gaps. Instead of guessing where your next client might come from, you can see the answer in the shape of your own network.
