# Note-Taking and Dictation

After every meeting or phone call, you know you should capture notes — but by the time you sit down to type, the details have faded and something else demands your attention.

SAM makes note capture so fast you'll actually do it. Every person's profile has inline note entry right where you need it. Prefer talking over typing? Tap the microphone button and dictate — SAM transcribes on-device and automatically cleans up grammar and filler words. You get rich text formatting with bold, italic, and image paste support, plus standard shortcuts like Command-S to save and Command-Z to undo.

The real magic happens after you save. SAM automatically analyzes your note and extracts action items you mentioned, life events (birthdays, job changes, new babies), topics discussed, and relationship mentions like family members or colleagues. You don't have to tag or organize anything — SAM handles it.

SAM knows when your scheduled meetings should end. Post-meeting prompts appear automatically showing the attendees and any pending action items. One click and you're capturing while the conversation is still fresh. Need to jot something down without leaving your current screen? Quick Note windows float above everything, always accessible.

Every note links back to the person and the meeting context, building a complete, searchable history of every interaction you've ever had. Six months from now, when a client mentions something that you captured in a note, you'll find it in seconds instead of digging through scattered files.
