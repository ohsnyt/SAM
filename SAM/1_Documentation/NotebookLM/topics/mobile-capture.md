# Mobile Capture

*Available on: iPhone (SAM Field)*

Most of your real conversations happen away from your desk — in coffee shops, at kitchen tables, at the office down the road. Trying to remember every detail later is a losing game, and pulling out a laptop kills the moment.

SAM Field turns your iPhone into a discreet capture device. Open the Record tab and the upcoming meeting from your calendar is already there, with attendee names pre-populated as participants. Tap the red button and you're recording. If your Mac is on the same WiFi, the audio streams there live — transcription and speaker diarization happen on your Mac while you talk. If your Mac isn't reachable, your phone records locally and queues the meeting; the moment you walk back into range, it syncs over with a progress bar.

After you stop, the meeting summary comes back to your phone — TL;DR, action items, decisions, follow-ups, life events that came up. If it looks right, tap Done. If something's off, swipe to delete and you don't need to walk back to your Mac. Picked the wrong meeting type? Change it and SAM regenerates the summary with the right prompt.

It's audio-only by design. No video, no awkward camera, no client wondering if you're recording their face. Just the conversation, turned into structured notes you can act on.

The benefit: you stop losing the details that matter. Whether you're across from a client or driving home from an event, the meeting gets captured and the follow-ups write themselves.
