# Content and Growth

For many people, social media platforms are useful tools to grow your business. But how? You know you should be posting on social media to attract leads and stay visible, but figuring out what to write, where to post, and whether it's compliant feels like a second job.

SAM's Grow section starts by analyzing your social profiles across LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, X, and Substack. Each gets a 0-100 score covering what's working, what needs improvement, content strategy suggestions, and network health. Think of it as a checkup for your online presence.

When it's time to create content, SAM suggests topics drawn from your recent client interactions and strategic analysis — real themes your audience cares about, not generic filler. It generates full drafts tailored to each platform: LinkedIn posts run 150-250 words with a professional tone, while Instagram captions stay punchy at 50-100 words. Writing posts becomes much easier.

Every draft goes through compliance scanning that flags guarantee language, return claims, and comparative statements before you post — catching issues that could create regulatory headaches.

SAM tracks your posting cadence and shows streaks to keep you consistent. Over time, SAM even learns your writing voice from your edits to your posts so drafts sound like you, not a robot.

The outcome: you build a visible, authentic online presence that generates inbound interest — without spending hours writing or worrying about compliance. Your content becomes a lead generation engine that works while you're in meetings.
