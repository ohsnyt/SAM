# Recruiting Pipeline

You're building a team, but tracking where each recruit stands — who's studying for their license, who's gone quiet, who just needs a push to get contracted — lives in your head or scattered across notes and texts.

SAM gives you a dedicated 7-stage recruiting pipeline built specifically for an independant agent building a team: Initial Conversation, Interest Expressed, Pre-Licensing, Licensing, Contracting, Active Agent, and Producing Agent. Each recruit has a visual stage selector right on their profile — click any stage to advance them, and SAM logs the transition with a timestamp you can never lose. That audit trail means you can look back months later and see exactly how long each person took to move through the funnel.

The real power is in the alerts. SAM tracks your mentoring cadence for every recruit and flags when someone drops off. Orange means they're starting to drift. You'll catch disengagement before it becomes a resignation. If you haven't connected with a recruit in over 21 days, their badge turns red. 

Your Business section includes a recruiting dashboard that shows funnel counts at each stage and how fast people are moving through. SAM calculates licensing completion rates so you can see patterns — maybe recruits who attend early training sessions finish licensing faster, or certain referral sources produce stronger agents.

The result: you stop losing recruits to neglect during the long licensing process. Every person on your team-building journey has a clear status, a contact cadence, and a history — so you can coach with confidence.

And the secret to helping their productivity? You give them their own SAM!
