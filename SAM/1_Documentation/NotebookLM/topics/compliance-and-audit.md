# Compliance and Audit

In financial services, one careless phrase in an email or social media post can trigger a compliance violation — and most advisors don't realize until it's too late.

SAM automatically scans every outgoing draft, social media post, and coaching communication for potential compliance issues across six categories: guarantees ("guaranteed return," "risk-free"), return or performance claims ("will earn," "beat the market"), promises ("I promise," "will ensure"), comparative claims ("better than"), suitability statements ("best for you"), and specific advice ("invest in," "buy this"). When SAM spots a flagged phrase, it highlights the issue and suggests a safer alternative right inline.

This is advisory only — SAM never blocks you from sending anything. You're always in control. And you can add your own custom compliance keywords if your broker-dealer has specific language restrictions.

Beyond content scanning, SAM maintains a complete audit trail of your business activity. Every pipeline stage transition — when a Lead becomes an Applicant, when an Applicant becomes a Client — is logged immutably with timestamps. Production records track full status history. And SAM's 30-day undo system keeps an immutable log of every destructive action, so you can always demonstrate what happened and when.

The result: you communicate confidently knowing SAM is watching for compliance pitfalls in real time, and you have the documentation to back it up if questions arise — all without adding a single extra step to your workflow.
