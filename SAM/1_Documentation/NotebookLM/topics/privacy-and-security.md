# Privacy and Security

You work with sensitive financial information and personal details every day. The last thing you need is an assistant that sends your client data to the cloud.

SAM keeps everything on your Mac. All AI processing happens on-device — no cloud servers, no telemetry, no data ever leaves your computer. When SAM analyzes an email or message thread, it reads the content, extracts the insights, and discards the raw text. Only the AI-generated summaries are stored, never the original messages. They stay in your Mail program where they belong.

Security starts at launch. SAM requires Touch ID or your system password every time you open it — no exceptions. If you step away from your desk, a configurable idle timeout re-locks the app automatically. When you copy sensitive information, SAM will auto-clear your clipboard after 60 seconds.

Your data stays protected at rest, too. SAM's backup system encrypts everything with a passphrase you set, so even a backup file sitting on an external drive is unreadable without your key. Access to your iMessage history and phone records uses Apple's security-scoped bookmarks, meaning SAM only reads what you've explicitly granted permission for.

SAM also respects boundaries around your data scope. It only monitors the email accounts and contact groups you designate as work-related — your personal accounts stay completely private.

The bottom line: you get a powerful coaching assistant with full access to your business communications, and absolute certainty that your clients' information never leaves your machine.
