# Relationship Coaching

You have dozens of clients, leads, and recruits — but no easy way to know who needs your attention right now, or what to say when you reach out.

SAM watches every interaction across your email, messages, calls, and calendar — not to snoop, but to build a living picture of each relationship. Every person gets a health score (green, yellow, orange, or red) based on engagement velocity: how frequently you're connecting and whether that pace is speeding up or slowing down. When someone starts going cold, SAM flags them before you've even noticed.

But SAM doesn't just tell you who — it tells you what to say and why it matters. Open a person's coaching preview and you'll see AI-generated talking points that reference your recent conversations, their life events (a birthday, a job change, a new baby), and where they sit in your pipeline. SAM even recommends the best communication channel for each person based on where they've been most responsive.

After every meeting, SAM prompts you to capture notes and automatically extracts action items and follow-ups. Forget to follow up with someone after a promising coffee chat? SAM won't.

The payoff: you stop losing warm relationships to neglect. Every outreach feels personal because it references real context. And because SAM connects individual follow-ups to your overall pipeline velocity, you can see how each conversation moves your business forward — not just the relationship.
