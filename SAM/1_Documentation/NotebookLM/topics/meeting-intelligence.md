# Meeting Intelligence

You walk into a client meeting and realize you can't remember what you discussed last time, what action items were pending, or that their daughter just graduated college.

SAM fixes this with intelligence before, during, and after every meeting. About fifteen minutes before a meeting starts, SAM automatically surfaces a pre-brief: attendee profiles, your full relationship history, recent interactions across email and messages, pending action items, life events worth mentioning, product holdings, and suggested talking points. You walk in prepared without lifting a finger.

After the meeting, SAM prompts you to capture notes — by typing or dictating. Its AI then extracts action items, detects life events mentioned in conversation, and generates follow-up suggestions. Everything links back to the person's record automatically.

Every meeting also gets a quality score from 0 to 100. SAM tracks whether you captured notes, how quickly you did it, whether action items were identified, if attendees were properly linked, and whether follow-ups were completed. Over time, you can see your meeting effectiveness improving.

The meeting card in your Today view expands automatically when a meeting is about to start, so the pre-brief is right there when you need it. No searching, no clicking around.

The result: you show up to every meeting looking like you remember everything (because SAM does), and nothing falls through the cracks afterward. Clients notice when you remember the details — it builds trust.
