# Business Dashboard

You know you're busy, but you can't easily answer: Is my pipeline healthy? Am I on pace for my goals? Where should I focus this week?

SAM's Business section gives you a clear, honest picture across four views. The Strategic tab shows scenario projections ("at your current prospecting pace, expect roughly 8 new clients in 6 months"), pattern insights ("Tuesday afternoon meetings produce 40% more follow-through"), and content ideas drawn from recent client conversations.

The Pipeline tab tracks both your client funnel (Lead to Applicant to Client) and your recruiting funnel through all seven stages. You can see conversion rates at each stage, how long people have been sitting in one place, and who's stalled. SAM flags stalled prospects so you can re-engage before they go cold.

The Production tab shows your policies and products by status — submitted, approved, pending, declined — along with product mix breakdowns and aging alerts for items that have been pending too long.

The Goals tab is where your targets are made specific to your context. Set a goal like "40 new clients this year" and SAM breaks it down into weekly and daily pacing with live progress bars. Pace badges show whether you're ahead, on track, or falling behind — right now, not at the end of the quarter.

Summary cards at the top give you an instant health check: active pipeline count, total clients, recruiting progress, and this month's production.

The outcome: you make strategic decisions based on real data instead of gut feel.
