# SAM: Your Strategic Advisory Mind

Independent financial strategists at WFG wear every hat at once — relationship builder, recruiter, business analyst, content creator, compliance officer — while trying to remember who you promised a callback, which recruit is stuck in licensing, and whether your pipeline is actually growing. The tools you have today make you do all the thinking.

SAM is a native Mac coaching assistant, paired with **SAM Field**, an iPhone companion. Together they watch your calendar, email, messages, and social activity, then tell you what to do next — specific people, specific scripts, specific reasons tied to your goals. Not a generic CRM. A personal strategist that understands both individual relationships and the big picture of your practice.

## It Knows Your People

Every morning, SAM greets you with a briefing built around your actual day. It surfaces the clients, leads, and recruits who need attention now — a renewal approaching, a prospect going cold, a recruit you have not contacted in two weeks. Before every meeting, SAM prepares you with attendee profiles, relationship history, and talking points. When someone mentions a life event, SAM coaches you on the right response. It drafts the outreach message, ready to send.

## It Sees Your Whole Business

SAM tracks your client pipeline from first conversation to closed business, flags stalling deals, and calculates conversion velocity. It does the same for recruiting. It breaks your goals into daily and weekly targets, analyzes how you spend your time, and spots patterns you cannot see on your own. Weekly digests give you a clear read on where your practice stands and where it is heading.

## It Stays Out of Your Way

Dictate a note after a meeting and SAM polishes it, extracts action items, and queues follow-ups. Paste or drag content from anywhere and SAM files it with the right contact. SAM suggests content topics based on what your clients are actually asking about and scans your drafts for compliance red flags before you post.

## It Goes With You

SAM Field captures the meetings and miles that happen away from your desk. Hit record before a client conversation: it streams to your Mac for live transcription on the same network, or records offline and syncs automatically when you are back in range. Trips track themselves — drive, stop, and SAM Field logs the route, distance, and addresses for IRS-compliant mileage. Your morning briefing follows you to your phone. Two devices, one practice.

## Your Data Never Leaves Your Devices

Everything happens on your Mac and iPhone. Notes, summaries, pipeline data, business metrics — never sent to a cloud server or third party. The two apps find each other through your private iCloud account: no codes, no QR scans. Backups are encrypted. Authentication is required on every launch. A full 30-day undo history protects you from mistakes.

## The Bottom Line

You stop reacting and start leading your practice. You know who to call, what to say, and how it connects to your growth trajectory — every day, at your desk and in the field.
