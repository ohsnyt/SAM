# SAM: Your Strategic Advisory Mind

Independent financial strategists at WFG wear every hat at once. You are the relationship builder, the recruiter, the business analyst, the content creator, and the compliance officer — all while trying to remember who you promised a callback, which recruit is stuck in licensing, and whether your pipeline is actually growing. The tools you have today make you do all the thinking. Nothing connects the dots.

SAM is a native Mac coaching assistant that watches your calendar, email, messages, and social activity, then tells you exactly what to do next — with specific people, specific scripts, and specific reasons tied to your business goals. It is not a generic CRM. It is a personal strategist that understands both your individual relationships and the big picture of your practice.

## It Knows Your People

Every morning, SAM greets you with a briefing built around your actual day. It surfaces the clients, leads, and recruits who need your attention right now — a policy renewal approaching, a prospect going cold, a recruit who has not heard from you in two weeks. Before every meeting, SAM prepares you with attendee profiles, relationship history, and talking points. When someone mentions a life event — a new baby, a retirement, a health concern — SAM coaches you on the right response. It tracks engagement streaks and relationship health across every channel so no one slips through the cracks. When it is time to reach out, SAM drafts the message for you, ready to send.

## It Sees Your Whole Business

SAM tracks your client pipeline from first conversation to closed business, flags deals that are stalling, and calculates how fast you are converting. It does the same for your recruiting pipeline. It breaks your goals into daily and weekly targets so you always know whether you are on pace. It analyzes how you spend your time, spots patterns you cannot see on your own, and suggests course corrections before small problems become big ones. Weekly strategic digests give you a clear read on where your practice stands and where it is heading.

## It Stays Out of Your Way

Capturing information is nearly effortless. Dictate a note after a meeting and SAM polishes it, extracts action items, and queues follow-ups automatically. Paste or drag content from anywhere and SAM files it with the right contact. Keyboard shortcuts keep you moving. SAM even suggests social media content topics based on what your clients are actually asking about and scans your drafts for compliance red flags before you post.

## Your Data Never Leaves Your Mac

Everything SAM does happens on your device. Your client notes, meeting summaries, pipeline data, and business metrics are never sent to a cloud server or shared with a third party. Backups are encrypted. Authentication is required every time you open the app. A full 30-day undo history protects you from mistakes.

## The Bottom Line

With SAM, you stop reacting and start leading your practice. You know exactly who to call, what to say, and how it connects to your growth trajectory — every single day. Available on Mac, with iPhone companion coming soon.
